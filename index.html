Standard rocket only. Recipe data from the Rust Wiki (rust.fandom.com), 904.83 update: 1 rocket = 2 metal pipe + 150 gun powder + 10 explosives · 1 explosive = 50 gun powder + 3 low grade fuel + 10 sulfur + 10 metal fragments · 1 gun powder = 2 sulfur + 2–3 charcoal.
Raid targets use commonly cited hard-side rocket counts — adjust to your wipe in the TARGETS constant. All recipe values live in the RECIPE object at the top of this file's script and are easy to edit.
Cheapest-boom data lives in the ENTITIES (HP + real per-hit damage of each explosive) and BOOM_METHODS (sulfur per unit) constants — edit after a wipe. The solver minimises sulfur to cross the HP line, comparing every single explosive and the cheapest two-explosive mix (e.g. soften with C4, finish with explosive ammo). Sulfur per explosive: rocket 1400, C4 2200, satchel 480, beancan 120, explosive 5.56 ammo 25. Per-hit damage from current Rust raid charts (rocket 220 to metal doors, 137 to walls; C4 275 to walls, 440 garage, 550 doors) — verify against your wipe.
